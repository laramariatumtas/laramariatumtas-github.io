About

Hi, I’m Lara Maria Tümtash, a 19-year-old college student who recently discovered an interest in UX/UI design. Although I’m new to the field and started with no prior knowledge, I’ve quickly become fascinated by the process of designing digital experiences that are both functional and visually engaging.

As I learn and grow, I’m exploring the fundamentals of user experience, interface design, and problem-solving through design. This portfolio represents the beginning of my journey—projects where I experiment, learn, and improve with every step.

How I Got Into
UX/UI

As someone who is still particularly young and unexperienced in work fields, i often found myself lost and unsatisfied with the idea of just going to college. I felt passive, and i wanted a second path in my life to walk on. I have always been creative, so i started thinking. “Should i get into some sort of design field?” And the answer to that question? Well i suppose this portfolio has already answered it.

Future
Goals

I’m committed to continuously improving my skills and learning more about UX/UI design with every project I create. As I grow and gain more experience, my goal is not only to become better at designing meaningful digital experiences, but also to share my ideas, designs, and knowledge with others. I hope that one day, when I’ve reached a level of mastery in this field, I can inspire and support other designers who are just beginning their journey, just like I am now.
