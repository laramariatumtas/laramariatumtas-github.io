Project Overview

As a part of my UX/Ui course with Figma, i created Monexa. An end-to-end digital wallet application.

This application is designed to help users pay using their mobile phones, and give them access to their credit card information and balance, purchase statistics, receive or send money to close friends, family and/or collegues etc.

Problem

A lot of users, especially older individuals, have a harder time tracking their spendings and have multiple cards in their possession. This is a challenge for most to keep their payment methods organized. They can lose track of their purchase history, credit card balance and so on.

Solution

Monexa’s main job is to simplify the user’s payment methods, making it so much easier for an individual to see all their purchase history, how much balance they have, whether it has increased or decreased, spending stats over the week etc.

Target Audience

Monexa is designed for individuals aged 18–45 who use digital payments and prefer managing their finances through mobile or web platforms. It targets users who value convenience, security, and efficiency when handling transactions. The audience includes students, professionals, and everyday users who want a simple and reliable way to send money, make payments, and keep track of their financial activity.

Goals & Objectives

Monexa main goal is to be a secure and user-friendly digital wallet that allows users to manage their money with ease. The goal is to simplify everyday financial tasks such as sending, receiving, and tracking payments, while ensuring strong security and reliability. The platform focuses on clear navigation, fast performance, and a seamless experience across all features. Monexa is designed to help users stay in control of their finances in one organized and accessible place.
