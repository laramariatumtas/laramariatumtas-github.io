Project Overview

This project was mostly inspired by my mother and her deep love for plants. Rootify is a plant care website designed to simplify how people understand and care for their plants. It provides clear, beginner-friendly guidance for both indoor and outdoor plants through a clean, accessible interface.

Problem

Many people struggle to keep plants alive due to confusing, scattered, or overly complex information. Beginners especially lack a simple, reliable place to learn essential plant care basics.

Solution

Rootify offers a streamlined experience with easy-to-navigate categories, simple care guides, and visually clear content. The design focuses on clarity and ease of use, helping users quickly find and understand how to care for their plants with confidence.

Target Audience

Rootify is designed for beginner to intermediate plant owners who are looking for simple, reliable guidance. The primary audience includes people with indoor plants, small outdoor spaces, or limited experience in plant care. Many users may feel overwhelmed by complex or scattered information, so the platform focuses on those who want quick, easy-to-understand answers to help their plants thrive.

Goals & Objectives

The goal of this project was to create a clean and intuitive platform that simplifies plant care information. The design focuses on clarity, accessibility, and ease of navigation, allowing users to quickly find what they need without feeling overwhelmed. A key objective was to present essential plant care knowledge in a visually organized and beginner-friendly way, while maintaining a calm and aesthetically pleasing experience.
