Project Overview

UchiGo is not just an online learning website, it’s also an opportunity for users to enhance and learn from all the courses UchiGo has to offer. It can help them learn a subject better for school, or perhaps gain knowledge and skill for a future job. The platform focuses on helping users discover and learn niche topics through clear learning paths and an intuitive user experience.

Problem

Many people in North Macedonia struggle to find accessible and structured online learning. Existing platforms are often overwhelming, not localized, or too focused on theory, which leaves users unsure where to start and how to progress.

Solution

UchiGo is a localized online learning platform designed to make skill-building simple and practical. It offers curated niche topics, clear learning paths, and an intuitive experience that helps users start quickly, stay engaged, and achieve real results.

Target Audience

UchiGo is designed for students and young adults aged 16–35 in North Macedonia who want to learn practical skills in a simple and structured way. It also targets individuals looking to improve their knowledge, explore new interests, or gain skills that can support their education or career development.

Goals & Objectives

UchiGo aims to simplify online learning by making it more accessible, structured, and engaging for users in North Macedonia. The goal is to help users quickly find relevant skills, follow clear learning paths, and stay motivated throughout their progress. The platform focuses on reducing friction, improving clarity, and delivering practical knowledge that leads to real outcomes.
