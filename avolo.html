Project Overview

Avolo was carefully created by my UX/UI professor and my group. A great website for people who live a healthy lifestyle, and prefer their skin care products, fashion, jewelry and food to be all natural.

We believe shopping should feel personal and conscious, not rushed or wasteful. That’s why Avolo focuses on small-batch production, natural ingredients, and durable materials designed to last. Our collection celebrates simplicity, authenticity, and the beauty of handmade goods.

Problem

Users who have a healthy and vegan lifestyle struggle to find truthful recipes, vegan clothing and jewelry and actual homemade skin care products. They are often given false claims that a product is fully vegan when in reality it’s not.

Solution

Avolo’s here to provide any individual who has dedicated their lifestyle to be a healthy and vegan one, providing users with authentic and all-natural products to use, as well as healthy food options that aren’t bland.

Target Audience

Avolo is designed for individuals aged 18–40 who are interested in healthy living, natural skincare, and unique handmade fashion and jewelry. It targets users who value authenticity, aesthetics, and mindful consumption in their everyday lifestyle.

Goals & Objectives

Avolo aims to create a simple and trustworthy platform for discovering and purchasing homemade, natural products. The goal is to highlight quality, support small creators, and encourage more mindful and conscious purchasing decisions. The platform focuses on providing a smooth and easy user experience, making it simple for users to browse products, find what they need, and return for future purchases. It is designed to build trust and encourage long-term use through clear design and reliable functionality.
