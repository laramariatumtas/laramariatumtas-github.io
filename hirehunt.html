Project Overview

HireHunt is a modern job-hunting platform designed to simplify the way users search and apply for jobs. The platform focuses on clarity, speed, and usability, allowing users to easily discover relevant opportunities, track applications, and navigate the job search process without feeling overwhelmed. It aims to create a more organized and user-friendly experience compared to traditional job platforms.

Problem

Many job seekers struggle to navigate complex and overwhelming job platforms. Listings are often cluttered, poorly filtered, and lack clarity, making it difficult for users to find relevant opportunities and keep track of their applications.

Solution

HireHunt is a modern job-hunting platform designed to simplify the job search process. It provides a clean and intuitive experience, helping users easily discover relevant roles, manage applications, and stay organized while searching for the right opportunity.

Target Audience

HireHunt is designed for job seekers, particularly students, recent graduates, and early-career professionals looking for accessible and efficient ways to find opportunities. It also supports users who may feel overwhelmed by traditional job platforms, offering a more simplified and user-friendly experience. The platform focuses on individuals who value clarity, speed, and a straightforward approach to job searching.

Goals & Objectives

The goal of this project was to create a streamlined job hunting platform that makes searching and applying for jobs more intuitive and less stressful. The design prioritizes clarity, easy navigation, and quick access to relevant listings. A key objective was to reduce friction in the job search process by organizing information in a clear, structured way, helping users focus on opportunities without unnecessary complexity.
